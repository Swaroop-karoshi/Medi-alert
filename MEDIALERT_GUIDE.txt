MediAlert: Application Overview & User Guide

MediAlert is a premium, minimalist healthcare application designed to bridge the gap between doctors and patients, ensuring reliable medication adherence through intelligent scheduling and fail-safe reminders.

---

Core Features

1. Dual-Role Architecture
The app tailors its interface based on whether you are a Doctor or a Patient, providing distinct tools for healthcare management and medication tracking.

2. Fail-Safe Medication Reminders (Patient)
- Persistent Alarms: Uses high-priority Android alarm managers to trigger even if the app is closed or the device is asleep.
- Insistent Notifications: Notifications act like a native clock alarm, requiring user interaction to ensure doses aren't missed.
- Quick Actions: "Taken", "Skipped", or "Snooze" directly from the lock screen or alarm interface.

3. Prescription Lifecycle Management
- Smart Creation (Doctor): Doctors select medicines from their inventory, set dosages, and link them to meal slots (Before/After Breakfast, etc.).
- Flexible Approval (Patient): Patients don't just "receive" a prescription; they approve it by setting their personal meal times, ensuring the schedule fits their lifestyle.
- Real-Time Sync: Schedules are synchronized via Supabase to keep both doctor and patient updated on adherence.

4. Inventory Tracking (Doctor)
- Stock Monitoring: Manage medication inventory with "Low Stock" and "Critical" alerts.
- Auto-Update: Stock levels can be configured to automatically decrement as prescriptions are issued.

5. Advanced Analytics & Reporting
- Adherence Charts: Visual reports showing "Taken" vs. "Missed" medications.
- Compliance Monitoring (Doctor): Doctors can view a specific patient's adherence report to track their recovery progress.

6. Battery & Stability Optimization
- Dedicated Background Stability dashboard to help users configure OEM-specific settings (like Samsung’s battery optimization) to ensure the alarm service remains active.

---

User Guide: How to Use MediAlert

For Doctors: Managing Your Practice
1. Onboarding: Sign up and select the "Doctor" role.
2. Inventory Setup: Go to the Inventory tab to add medications you commonly prescribe. Set "Low Stock" thresholds to get notified when you need to restock.
3. Connecting with Patients:
   - Navigate to Patient Management.
   - Use your unique Doctor ID or QR Code to invite patients to your network.
4. Prescribing:
   - Select a patient and tap Add Prescription.
   - Add medicine items, set the dosage (e.g., "1 Tablet"), and select the meal slot (e.g., "After Breakfast").
   - Submit the prescription. It will stay "Pending" until the patient approves it.

For Patients: Staying Healthy
1. Onboarding: Sign up and select the "Patient" role.
2. Connecting with your Doctor: 
   - Accept the invitation from your doctor in the Home dashboard.
3. Approving Medication:
   - When you see a "Pending Prescription," tap on it.
   - Review the medicines and Configure Your Schedule by choosing exactly what time you eat breakfast, lunch, and dinner.
   - Tap Pay & Approve (if applicable) to activate your alarms.
4. Managing Doses:
   - When an alarm sounds, view the medicine and doctor details.
   - Mark it as Taken immediately to update your progress.
   - If you missed a dose, you can manually update its status in the Home timeline.
5. Analytics: Check the Analytics tab to see your weekly adherence score. Aim for 100%!

---

Technical Stability Tip
To ensure you never miss a dose, go to Settings > Background Stability and follow the instructions to "Disable Battery Optimization" and "Allow Auto-launch". This ensures the MediAlert alarm system remains "Always-On" in the background.
